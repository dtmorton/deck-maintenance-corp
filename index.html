  • Wood Cleaning

    Properly cared for wooden decks and fences add to the value and appearance of a home. Timely cleaning and staining or sealing of exterior wood structures will keep them looking like new. The restoration process begins with a thorough cleaning and or stripping as determined by the type of wood and its previous finish history. Even high quality stains and sealers will be unable to live up to their expected performance if they are applied over a poorly prepared surface.

  • Wood Treatment

    Once the wood surface has been properly cleaned, and stripped if necessary, it is time to apply the chosen sealant. Some of the biggest marketing fibs ever told are in the sealant industry. Many businesses fail each year because they try to warrant their jobs based on the written warranty on the can of sealant. Deck Maintenance Corp regularly tests sealants, stains and preservatives against the warranty from the manufacturer and then provides those results to the homeowner.
